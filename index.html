How We Work

Our Simple 4-Step Process

From first inquiry to final walkthrough — we make it easy.

1

Initial Consultation

Send us your inquiry via Viber or email. Share your requirements, layout, or photos, and we’ll discuss your needs, budget, and project details.

2

Design & Planning

Using SketchUp, we create a detailed 3D design based on your measurements and specifications for your review and approval.

3

Fabrication & Installation

Once approved, our team carefully fabricates your modular cabinets and completes on-site installation with precision and efficiency.

4

Project Turnover

We conduct a final inspection to ensure everything meets our quality standards before officially turning over the completed project to you.
